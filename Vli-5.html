175/80-16(6.95-16) VLI-5


All-season tire VLI-5 for light trucks for driving on asphalt roads and unpaved roads. It has a non-directional bias-symmetric tread pattern that provides excellent traction, controllability, quick braking on any surface. The deep longitudinal groove and numerous broken grooves guarantee a quick drainage of water and dirt from the contact area, preventing aquaplanning.Massive lugs improve the tires cross-country ability.
